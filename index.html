HOW TO MAKE FRIED RICE

Ingredients

  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • 2 eggs and 5 meatballs
  • 1 plate of white rice
  • 1 tea spoon of salt
  • 1/2 stick of leeks
  • 3 chilies (chilie sauce, soy sauce, and paper
  • 1 tablespoon of cooking oil
  • tomato, lettuce, and cucumber

Tools

  • stove
  • frying pan
  • spatula
  • plate
  • spoon
  • mortar and pestle
  • knife
  • chopping board

steps

  1. First of all, puree the garlic, onion, and chilies with the help of pestle and mortar.
  2. Next, prepare a frying pan and then pour the cooking oil to the pan according to your taste.
  3. After that, heat the frying pan just using a medium flame.
  4. After the cooking oil is hot enough, pour the puree seasoning.
  5. Stir all the materials until fragrant and ripe
  6. Then put the eggs and meatballs. Scramble it until cooked.
  7. Now add the 1 plate of white rice and stir it until mixed enough with all ingredients.
  8. Add salt, chili sauce, soy sauce, stick of leeks and pepper until all materials are blended.
  9. After everything on the pan is fragrant enough, serve the fried rice into the plate.
  10. Now you can decorate it with sliced tomato, lettuce, and cucumber to make your fried rice looks more delicious and interesting
  11. Your fried rice is ready to served.
